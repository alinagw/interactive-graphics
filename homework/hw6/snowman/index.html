Snowman

Challenge: Remake Classmate's Sketch

Looking through my classmates' sketches, I was really drawn to Jacqueline's aesthetic sketch with the four stacked black 3D cubes rotating at different speeds on a red background. I liked the simplicity of the sketch, but it was still so visually striking. The stacked cubes immediately reminded me of... a snowman! I wanted to recreate her sketch as my own imagined snowman. In complete contrast to her distressing black and red color scheme, this happy little fella is a much friendlier image. I tried to capture the stacking and rotation components from her piece and recreate it in a more playful manner.

Instructions:

  • Watch and enjoy!
  • If you want to stop the snowman's rotation to see what it looks like from the front, press and hold the mouse button.
