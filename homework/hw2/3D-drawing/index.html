Draw in 3D

Challenge: Draw in Different Spaces

I wanted to implement different drawing apps in different "spaces," challenging myself to interpret what "space" means. In this sketch, I created a drawing app in 3D space. You can draw strokes with your mouse that exist in a 3D world that can be rotated. That way, you can draw on several planes to construct a 3D object viewable from (almost) every angle.

Instructions:

  • Draw by clicking and dragging with your mouse on the canvas
  • Use the left and right arrow keys to rotate your drawing around the y-axis in 3D space
  • Use number keys 0 - 9 to change the brush color
