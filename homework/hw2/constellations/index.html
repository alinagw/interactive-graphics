Create Constellations

Challenge: Draw in Different Spaces

I wanted to implement different drawing apps in different "spaces," challenging myself to interpret what "space" means. In this sketch, you can draw in actual space! The stars in the night sky are generated randomly on each refresh. You can connect the dots to create constellations out of the star patterns. Each time you play will be different.

Instructions:

  • Draw a line between two stars by clicking with your mouse on the first star and clicking the second star you want to connect it to
  • To cancel drawing a line, click on empty space
