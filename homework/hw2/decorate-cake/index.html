Decorate Your Own Birthday Cake

Challenge: Draw in Different Spaces

I wanted to implement different drawing apps in different "spaces," challenging myself to interpret what "space" means. In this sketch, instead of drawing in 3D space, you are drawing on a 3D object in space. I wanted to make it a fun experience, so I transformed a simple box into a birthday cake. You can decorate the sides and the top however you want, but unfortunately it isn't actually edible...

Instructions:

  • Draw by clicking and dragging with your mouse on the canvas
  • Select which part of the cake to draw on using the drop down, choose either "side" or "top"
  • Change the color of your brush using the drop down
